# Personalized Daily Workout Module

## Goal
Develop a personalized daily workout module for hermes-voice that provides engaging, tailored 20-minute workouts suitable for beginners, requiring no equipment, and designed to be completed in the mornings before work.

## Context
Hermes-voice has a history of creating interactive and personalized learning modules, indicating a preference for engaging, customized experiences. The user has previously worked on a language learning module that included interactive lessons and speech recognition, suggesting a familiarity with and preference for technology-driven, personalized solutions. The user has expressed a need for a beginner-level workout routine that fits into a busy morning schedule, emphasizing the importance of convenience and accessibility.

## Requirements
1. **Personalization Engine**: Implement an AI-based system to tailor daily workouts to the user's fitness level and preferences, ensuring variety and progression.
2. **User Interface**: Design a user-friendly interface that allows easy navigation and interaction, optimized for mobile platforms.
3. **Workout Tracking**: Include a feature to track completed workouts, progress over time, and provide feedback to motivate the user.
4. **Gamification Elements**: Integrate gamification features such as badges, streaks, or challenges to enhance user engagement and retention.
5. **Morning Routine Optimization**: Ensure the module is optimized for quick access and completion in the morning, with a focus on efficiency and minimal setup.
6. **No Equipment Workouts**: Develop a library of workouts that require no equipment, focusing on bodyweight exercises suitable for beginners.
7. **Time Management**: Ensure each workout is strictly 20 minutes to fit the user's time constraints.

## Non-goals
- Developing a comprehensive fitness app with advanced features like nutrition tracking or social networking.
- Creating workouts that require gym equipment or advanced fitness levels.
- Implementing a web-based platform, as the focus is on mobile optimization.

## Success Criteria
- The user successfully completes daily workouts with ease and reports increased motivation and satisfaction with the personalized approach.
- The module consistently delivers 20-minute workouts that fit seamlessly into the user's morning routine.
- Positive user feedback on the interface's usability and the effectiveness of the personalization engine.
- Increased user engagement through gamification elements, evidenced by regular usage and achievement of in-app milestones.
