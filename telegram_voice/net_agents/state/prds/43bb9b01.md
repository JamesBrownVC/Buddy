# Chinese Learning Module for Hackathon

## Goal
Develop an innovative Chinese learning module that utilizes voice technology to enhance language acquisition, tailored for a hackathon demo pitch by 'hermes-voice' on July 18, 2026.

## Context
The user, 'hermes-voice', is preparing for a hackathon demo, indicating a need for a novel and engaging approach to language learning. The user's name suggests a focus on voice technology, which could be leveraged to create an interactive and immersive learning experience. The lack of specific research results on current methods implies that the module should explore unique or underutilized techniques in the realm of voice-assisted language learning.

## Requirements
1. **Voice Interaction**: Implement a feature that allows users to practice pronunciation and receive feedback using voice recognition technology.
2. **Gamification**: Incorporate game-like elements such as points, levels, and rewards to motivate users and enhance engagement.
3. **Cultural Context**: Include cultural insights and context to help users understand the nuances of the Chinese language and its usage.
4. **Progress Tracking**: Provide a system for users to track their learning progress over time, with visual indicators of improvement.
5. **User-Friendly Interface**: Design an intuitive and accessible user interface that facilitates easy navigation and interaction.
6. **Offline Mode**: Ensure the module can be used offline to accommodate users with limited internet access.
7. **Hackathon-Ready Demo**: Prepare a concise and compelling demonstration of the module's capabilities for the hackathon pitch.

## Non-goals
- Do not create a comprehensive language course; focus on a specific aspect or feature that showcases innovation.
- Avoid developing a text-based learning module; the emphasis should be on voice interaction.
- Do not include advanced linguistic theories or complex grammar explanations; keep the content simple and engaging.

## Success Criteria
- The module successfully recognizes and provides feedback on user pronunciation in real-time.
- Users report increased motivation and engagement due to gamification elements.
- The hackathon demo effectively showcases the module's unique features and potential impact on language learning.
- Positive user feedback on the cultural context provided, indicating a deeper understanding of the language.
- The interface receives favorable reviews for its ease of use and accessibility.
- Users can access and use the module offline without significant loss of functionality.
